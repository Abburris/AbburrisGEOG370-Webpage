The first step was to add the Natural Earth shape file to my QGIS document. Then I used to Indicatrix Mapper pluggin to generate Tissot circles. I generated the different projections by changing the coordinate reference system on QGIS.

WGS84 Projection - 4326

This projection distorts distance, but it preserves angels and directions. It is used to determine direction while navigating. There is no distortion at the equator, however the distortion increases closer to the poles.

Aitoff Projection

This is a modified azimuthal projection. It distorts area, distance, and direction. The Equator and Prime Meridian are both strait, however, the latitude and longitude become increasingly curved closer to the poles.
